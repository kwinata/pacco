*****************
Welcome to Pacco!
*****************

.. image:: _images/pacco.png

With Pacco, you can upload and download your prebuilt binaries (or even dependency source files!) in an organized way
for building your project for any language. This documentation will contain introduction about Pacco, how to use it,
how it works and how to extend it.
